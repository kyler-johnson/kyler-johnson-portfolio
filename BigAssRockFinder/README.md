## Big Ass Rock Finder (B.A.R.F.)

The B.A.R.F. is a scintillation detector that utilizes a Hamamatsu R1513 photomultiplier tube, five-inch organic scintillator, and zinc-sulfide screen. It is designed specifically to quickly detect Naturally Occurring Radioactive Material (NORM) in large low-background environments. 

The housing components were all designed in Solidworks and printed on an Ender 5 Plus 3D-Printer with PLA filament.
